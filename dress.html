What to Wear


    There is a dress code at NLC that members must adhere to or face consequence. The professional dress code (for genereral sessions, all competitive events, campaign rally, caucuses, workshops, and National Leadership Academy) goes as follows:


  • Dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  • Pant or skirt suit
  • Suit, dress shirt, and tie
  • Dress skirt or dress slacks with coordinated blouse and/or sweater
  • Sport coat, coordinated dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  • BPA blazer with dress skirt or dress slacks with blouse or sweater
  • BPA blazer with dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie

For more info, please check out this PDF for the official dress guidelines.
